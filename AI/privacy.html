Privacy Policy

The legal stuff — but we promise it's actually readable. No legalese, no tricks, no shadiness. Just vibes and transparency.

⚡

TL;DR — The Quick Version

  • 🙅 We don't sell your data. Ever.
  • 💾 Your chats live in your browser, not our servers.
  • 🔍 We don't track you, stalk you, or profile you.
  • 🌐 Web search queries might touch a third-party search API.
  • 😌 It's a small project run by humans who also hate creepy data practices.

Storage: Your data lives at home

Everything you type into OLM — your chat history, display name, profile picture, settings — is stored using localStorage on your own device. We literally cannot see it. Your conversations never leave your machine unless you send a message to our backend for an AI response.

AI Messages: Fresh requests only

When you hit send, your message travels to our backend server. We do not log, store, or archive your messages after the response is sent. The backend is a fresh request each time — no persistent user identity on our end.

Cookies & Tracking

No analytics trackers. No third-party ad network pixels. OLM doesn't use cookies at all — preference storage is all localStorage, which stays on your device.

Optional Google Sign-In

If you sign in with Google, we use your public profile info only to display your avatar and name inside the app — locally. We don't store it on a server.

Contact

Got questions? Hit us up on Discord. We're a small crew and we actually respond.
